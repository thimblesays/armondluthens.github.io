about my work

I am interested in the full stack.

A majority of my work is done in Java and Java Swing Components, and much of my code can be found on github. I primarily work out of eclipse or netbeans, and the command line is an integral part of my life. While working on side projects, on top of coursework during the academic year is tough, I believe it is absolutely necessarily for becoming a well-rounded developer. I enjoy building sites for others professionally, as well as in my free time to feed my artistic voids.
